Our Physical Therapists work in conjunction with our Neurologist and Orthopedic Surgeon to design patient rehabilitation from injury or illness.

Using a combination of Manual Therapy and Education, a Physical Therapist can assist you in returning you back to safely perform your daily tasks after an injury, heal soft tissues with a combination of massage, manipulation and mobilization of bones, joints and tissues.

Functional Capacity Evaluation (FCE)

Our Physical Therapist uses a variety of tools, techniques and instruments to measure the physical abilities of a patient before and after an injury (i.e., work injury). FCEs are used:

  • to improve the likelihood that the patient will be safe in subsequent job performance

  • determine the presence and if so, the degree of disability so that a bureaucratic or juridical entity can assign, apportion, or deny financial and medical disability benefits.
