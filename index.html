About Us

Serving Louisville from three convenient locations, our health providers offer specialized medical care for those injured at an accident at work, in a motor-vehicle or while playing sports. Our expertise in Neurology, Orthopedics, Physical Therapy, Chiropractic and Massage Therapy provides conservative therapy for those seeking sustainable pain management, muscle strengthening and recovery.

Most healthcare, insurance accepted

We accept most major healthcare, workers comp and automobile insurance companies. In many cases, patients injured at work or in a motor vehicle accident don't need any healthcare insurance. Please click here to learn more about no-fault laws in the state of Kentucky.

Transportation Assistance

We provide complimentary transportation from your doorstep to ours for eligible patients. Please contact us for more information.
