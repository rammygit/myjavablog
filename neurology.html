Dr. Blaine Lisner, a neurologist, and former neurosurgeon completed his training in Neurology at Ohio State University and additional training in Neurophysiology from the Mayo Clinic.

He treats a variety of neurological ailments, including: bullet Cognitive and behavioral disorders bullet Memory loss including Mild Cognitive Impairment and Alzheimer's disease bullet Frontotemporal dementia syndromes including Pick's disease bullet Parkinsonian dementia syndromes (Lewy Body disease) bullet Head injury and post-concussion syndromes bullet Migraine disorders and atypical pain syndromes (Dejerine-Roussy and trigeminal neuralgia) bullet Multiple sclerosis and variants, including confirmation of the diagnosis bullet Pain Management including Epidural Steroid Injections, Facet Blocks, Nerve Blocks bullet Neurological Testing including NCV/ EMG

He also treats most general neurology complaints, including stroke, epilepsy, sleep disorders and neck and back injury. In conjunction with Physical Therapy, he treats patients injured in Motor Vehicle Accident as well as Industrial and Workmen's Compensation patients.
