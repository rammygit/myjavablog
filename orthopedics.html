Dr. Anthony McEldowney, an orthopedic surgeon with more than 16 years of experience, specializes in injury-related treatment for broken bones and ligament injuries. He is also an expert on arthritis-related issues, and can administer deep facet block injections to take care of acute pain shortly after an injury. This also helps the patient to achieve muscle strengthening and work-hardening by performing active therapy exercises with our physical therapy.

Independent Medical Exams

Dr. McEldowney is a Certified Independent Medical Examiner, with several years of experience of providing Impairment Rating as per the AMA guidelines. Please call our clinic for a copy of our physician bio.
